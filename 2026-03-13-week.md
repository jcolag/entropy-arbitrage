[<i class="fab fa-mastodon"></i>](https://mastodon.social/@jcolag/116199344429116133) [Free 10-minute online programs aimed at overcoming depression led to real improvements](https://theconversation.com/free-10-minute-online-programs-aimed-at-overcoming-depression-led-to-real-improvements-new-research-272493) from The Conversation @TheConversationUS@newsie.social

> These monthlong gains were small on average – around a 4% greater reduction on a standard depression measure for the top two exercises compared with the control – but small average effects can make a real difference, especially because these programs’ free, brief nature gives them a unique ability to reach people at a global scale.

Hashtags:  #Depression #Therapy

To my knowledge, I don't suffer from depression, but I did step through the top two online tools to better understand what this all means.  As such, a word of warning to people who might benefit from this, the intellectual tools do seem helpful, but the software tools to teach/demonstrate the techniques felt somewhere between simplistic and assuming that you've had experience dealing with and talking about these issues.  Plus, it really makes a pamphlet interactive for no real reason...
