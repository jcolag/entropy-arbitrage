## What Works Well?

I suppose that you can probably guess from the project description that the film does flow fairly well, motion capture generally (as the selling point) making characters move realistically.

## What Works...Less Well?

{% include lesswell.md %}

The film feels...*inconsistent* to me.  We have our three scenes, where the first and third look like a video game, but the middle has moments that look almost photorealistic.  And in that second scene, the fight seems to have strange lighting, where more sunlight hits the ground than anything above the ground.

It similarly feels awkward to go for high-concept science fiction, here, but have the main setting look like it takes place in probably Southeast Asia.  I don't want to say that such a thing couldn't happen in a fictional world, but it gives the impression that everything probably takes place on Earth, which then feels like it demands some explicit internal logic.
